About StudyBuddy

StudyBuddy is a web-based productivity tool designed to help students and learners effectively plan and manage their study routines. It features an intuitive weekly calendar where users can add, delete, and mark tasks as completed.

The application supports dark and light themes for comfortable viewing, and users can export their schedule as a PDF or CSV file. Our planner enhances focus, organization, and efficiency, making it easier to track academic goals and improve time management.

Whether preparing for exams, completing assignments, or setting personal milestones,StudyBuddy is your companion in achieving academic excellence.
